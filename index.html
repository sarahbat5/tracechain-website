How it works

Two roles. One shared truth.

The entire flow happens on the Sepolia blockchain through Alchemy — both registration and verification are on-chain transactions, not database lookups.

01
Manufacturer registers a product
Enter product details in the Manufacturer Portal. Clicking "Register to blockchain" calls the smart contract's registerProduct() function and permanently stores the data on-chain.
Manufacturer Portal
02
A unique QR code is generated
After registration, TraceChain generates a unique authentication code (e.g. TC-A1B2C3D4) and a downloadable QR code. Print it and attach it to the product packaging.
Manufacturer Portal
03
Customer scans or enters the code
The customer uses the Customer Portal to scan the QR code with their camera, or manually types the authentication code. No app installation required.
Customer Portal
04
Authenticity is verified on-chain
TraceChain queries the smart contract directly. If the code exists, full product details are returned and the product is confirmed authentic. If not found, a counterfeit warning is shown.
